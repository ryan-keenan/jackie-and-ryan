Our Story

Our journey began on a crisp autumn day in San Francisco, when a chance encounter at a coffee shop on Valencia Street turned into a three-hour conversation about everything from travel dreams to our shared love of hiking. What started as a simple coffee date quickly blossomed into weekend adventures exploring the Bay Area's hidden trails and coastal paths.

Over the years, we've traveled to four continents together, caught sunrise from mountain peaks, and even witnessed a total solar eclipse side by side. Ryan proposed at our favorite spot in Lake Tahoe, the same place we'd watched our first sunset together. About Chef Tammy Huynh

Chef-proprietor Tammy Huynh brings a unique perspective to Vietnamese cuisine. With a doctorate in pharmacy from the University of Pacific and a background in biochemistry, she transitioned from science to the culinary arts, following in the footsteps of her mother who pioneered authentic Vietnamese cuisine in the Bay Area.

Since opening Tamarine in 2002, Chef Huynh has created a menu that showcases modern Vietnamese cuisine with refined innovation, utilizing traditional Vietnamese ingredients in new compositions that reflect California's abundant produce and culinary culture.

The Cuisine

Expect a sophisticated contrast of textures and exotic flavors, drawing from Vietnamese traditions influenced by Chinese, Cambodian, Laotian, Thai, and French cuisines. The full bar program features carefully curated California wines and craft cocktails designed with Vietnamese ingredients.
