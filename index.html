Venue

About - Tamarine is Vietnamese restaurant located in Palo Alto, and features an art gallery displaying the works of artists throughout Vietnam. Executive Chef Tammy Huynh has a long legacy in the restaurant industry; her mom opened Vung Tau restaurant in downtown San Jose in 1985, one of the pioneering Vietnamese restaurants in the area.

Parking - Behind the restaurant is a parking lot with free parking.

Our Story

We met in a climbing gym in Reno, NV. It was the height of the pandemic, November 2020, so we had our masks on but managed to make a connection. We had both moved to Reno recently as newly remote tech workers.

Over the years since then we have been on adventures around lake Tahoe and the eastern Sierra, and we've traveled together to Mexico, Colombia, Taiwan, and Korea.

We've settled in now in Reno with a flock of chickens, favorite local restaurants, bike trails, cross-country skiing, the lake, and the river.
