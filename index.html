Our Story

We met in a climbing gym in Reno, NV. It was the height of the pandemic, November 2020, so we had our masks on but managed to make a connection. We had both moved to Reno recently as newly remote tech workers.

Over the years since then we have been on adventures around lake Tahoe and the eastern Sierra, and we've traveled together to Mexico, Colombia, Taiwan, and Korea.

We've settled in now in Reno with a flock of chickens, favorite local restaurants, bike trails, cross-country skiing, the lake, and the river.

About Chef Tammy Huynh

Chef-proprietor Tammy Huynh brings a unique perspective to Vietnamese cuisine. With a background in biochemistry, she transitioned from science to the culinary arts. She follows in the footsteps of her mother Chac Do, who opened the Vung Tau restaurant in downtown San Jose in 1985, one of the pioneering authentic Vietnamese restaurants in the region.

Since opening Tamarine in 2002, Chef Huynh has created a menu that showcases modern Vietnamese cuisine influenced by Chinese, Cambodian, Laotian, Thai, French and Californian food culture.
